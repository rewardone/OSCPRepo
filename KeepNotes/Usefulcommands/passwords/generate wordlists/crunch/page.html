Crunch for use online and offline. Generate wordlists according to your requirements. Give max/min length and provide a character set.

Syntax: crunch <min> <max> <character-set> -t <patter> -o <path>
Pattern is optional

Example: crunch 3 4 ignite -o dict.txt
Will create dictionary min 3 letters, max 4 letters, using only characters found in 'ignite' and output to dict.txt
