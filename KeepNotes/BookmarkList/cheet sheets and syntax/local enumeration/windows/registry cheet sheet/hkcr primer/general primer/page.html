HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT is just a combination of HKLM:\Software\Classes and HKCU:\Software\Classes
Due to the fact that these are merged, you can "hijack" keys in HKCR:\CLSID by adding keys in HKCU:\Software\Classes\CLSID.

A lot of things (such as scheduled tasks) pull from HKCR:\CLSID for actions
COM objects will load from HKCR:\CLSID.
